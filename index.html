About Our School

Building Strong Foundations Since 2012

Samskruti Preschool, operating under the Kidzee brand, has been a part of the Jubilee Hills community for over a decade. Located in a prime residential area, our school provides structured early education that blends Montessori methods with an integrated curriculum designed to develop cognitive, social, and emotional skills.

With a maximum capacity of 30 children and a 1:4 teacher-student ratio, every child receives individual attention in a safe, CCTV-monitored environment with air-conditioned classrooms.
